## Framework version drift

Saudi regulations evolve. Frameworks, implementing regulations, control catalogues, reporting timelines, penalty bands, and licence-class definitions can be amended without long notice. The Skill notes the framework versions it relies on (see `SKILL.md` §2), but **users must verify the current published version** of any framework or regulation before relying on a finding for formal use.

The Skill is configured to **flag uncertain control numbers** with `[verify ref]` rather than fabricate. This is a deliberate guardrail; it does not eliminate the need for the user to confirm citations against official documents.

## Quality of output depends on quality of input

The Skill assesses what the user describes. Incomplete or inaccurate scenarios produce incomplete or inaccurate findings. The Skill cannot:

- Read your live systems, configurations, logs, or evidence repositories.
- Confirm whether a stated control is actually operating effectively.
- Verify regulator correspondence, licence status, or filed reports.
- Detect omissions in user-supplied descriptions.
